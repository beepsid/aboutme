About me

I'm a Computer Science graduate and completed Btech in 2025. Technology has been something I've been genuinely drawn to, not just as a subject, but as a way to enjoy, explore and make sense of the world around us.

Now that my degree is done, I'm trying to channel that curiosity into the world of data. My goal is to grow into a Data Analyst and Business Analyst and someday become someone who can bridge the gap between raw numbers and meaningful decisions. I find it fascinating how the right analysis can completely change the way a business thinks and acts.

I'm always looking to sharpen my skills, explore data and surf the internet to explore new things, and build things that actually matter to the people I want them to matter. The journey is just getting started.
