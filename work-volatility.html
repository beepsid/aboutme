002 : Market Volatility & Safe-Haven Correlation Engine

An end-to-end financial data pipeline analyzing the relationship between Nifty 50 equity volatility and Gold ETF performance during market stress periods. Built to demonstrate a full data engineering workflow: Python extraction, SQL modeling, and Power BI visualization.

Stack: Python · PostgreSQL · SQL · Power BI · yfinance

Key Finding: Gold showed positive or flat returns on the majority of Nifty crash days (daily drop > 1.5%), confirming its safe-haven behavior during high-volatility periods.
